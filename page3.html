PROJECT 2: EXHIBITIONISTS
Some complex machines don't come with detailed instructions on how exactly the machine works.
My group members and I collected and gathered a lot of information, and put them together to make a rough user experience map.
Sufficient preparation is essential to complete a project. The more problems you discover in the process of preparation, the more problems you will find. So it is necessary.

 I created persona based on my researches and interviews, I divided several groups into different purpose of museum-visiting.
Some of them are really crazy about science and museum, and the other group just like “finish a task”or just have a look.

summary:

Finally, I made a handbook through my collection and investigation, which really helped me open the gate of communication design, and also let me know that user-centered design needs repeated elaboration and modification to create a satisfying design.
